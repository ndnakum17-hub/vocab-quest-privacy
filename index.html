Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 3, 2026  |  Developed by: Devisha Foundation  |  Last Updated: April 3, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to ITI Vocab Quest, developed by Devisha Foundation. This app is designed to help ITI students across Gujarat learn English vocabulary in a simple, fun, and effective way using their native Gujarati language.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the app's features:

Optional information provided by you:

  • Student Name (optional — you can use the app without providing this)
  • ITI Institution Name (optional)
  • Trade / Course Name (optional)

Information stored only on your device:

  • Your quiz results and progress
  • Bookmarked words
  • Which word sets you have completed
  • Your app preferences (dark mode, etc.)

We do NOT collect:

  • Passwords or any login credentials
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or any device files
  • Any sensitive personal information

🎯 How We Use Your Information

If you choose to provide your name, ITI name, and trade name, this information is recorded in our Google Sheet database solely for the purpose of understanding which ITI institutions are using the app, so we can improve content for students.

  • We do not sell your information to any third party
  • We do not use your information for marketing or advertising targeting
  • Your quiz results and bookmarks are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any server
  • Providing personal information is completely optional — the app works fully without it

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Device advertising ID
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • App usage patterns for ad relevance

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings under Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

💾 Data Storage & Security

  • All your progress, bookmarks, and quiz results are stored locally on your device using secure device storage
  • Optional student information (name, ITI, trade) is stored in a Google Sheet accessible only to Devisha Foundation
  • Vocabulary word data is fetched from a read-only Google Sheet and cached on your device
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data we receive
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device permanently

👨‍🎓 Students & Young Users

ITI Vocab Quest is designed for students aged 15 years and above, which aligns with the typical age of ITI students in India. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 is using this app and has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete the information promptly.

🔗 Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets API — to fetch vocabulary word data and store optional student info. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and updates.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

✅ Your Rights

  • You can use the app completely without providing any personal information
  • You can request deletion of any information you have provided by contacting us
  • You can clear all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your Android device settings

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
